4. Evidence Regarding Quantum Advantage

Conclusion: No evidence for quantum advantage. While QAOA and Hybrid QAOA find valid, high-quality solution schedules, classical Exact and Local Search solvers execute in under 1 millisecond, yielding equivalent or lower objectives. At N=5 variables, classical search is globally optimal and significantly faster. Quantum advantage is not present at this problem scale.
