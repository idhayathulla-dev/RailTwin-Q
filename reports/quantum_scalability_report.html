Key Structural Scaling Insights

  • Exact Solver Timeout: At $N \ge 30$, brute-force search space complexity exceeds $2^30 pprox 10^9$ possibilities. Computational overhead and runtime grows exponentially, leading to execution timeouts.
  • QAOA Simulator Memory Bound: Classical statevector emulation requires $2^N$ complex floats. While N=20 variables fits in memory easily, $N \ge 30$ causes simulator RAM bottlenecks, requiring hardware quantum QPUs.
  • Classical Heuristics Robustness: Greedy and Simulated Annealing execute in under 10 milliseconds even at $N=100$ variables, finding high-quality solutions with low energy.
